# **CultivarID: The Digital Product Passport for Rare Botanicals**

**CultivarID** is the premier Digital Product Passport (DPP) and inventory management application designed exclusively for the high-end rare plant market. Operating as the B2B companion app to the RarePlantVendors directory, CultivarID bridges the gap between physical botanical events and verifiable digital provenance.

## **Core Capabilities:**

* **Digital Provenance & Verification:** Replaces fragile paper tags with immutable digital ledgers. CultivarID tracks a specimen's complete history, including genetic origin (e.g., sport variegation), propagation method (tissue culture vs. stem cutting), and mother-plant verification.  
* **Dynamic QR Code Generation:** Empowers vendors to instantly generate printable, durable QR care tags for their physical event booths or pop-up shops.  
* **Immersive Customer Discovery:** When an attendee scans a CultivarID tag, they are instantly immersed in a mobile-optimized, high-end visual experience detailing the plant's high-res imagery, botanical lineage, and precise care instructions.  
* **Real-Time POS & Inventory Sync:** Operates as a seamless point-of-sale utility for vendors on the event floor. As attendees scan and purchase items, CultivarID automatically deducts live stock and synchronizes directly with the vendor's central dashboard.
